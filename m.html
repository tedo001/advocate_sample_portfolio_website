About

COUNSEL
WITH conviction

Alice Fernandes is a Senior Advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Maharashtra and practising before the Supreme Court of India, Bombay High Court, and all major Tribunals. Her practice is broad by design — she believes exceptional advocacy comes from mastering the full spectrum of law, not just one narrow field.

Educated at Government Law College, Mumbai and trained further at the London School of Economics, Alice combines sharp academic grounding with a natural instinct for high-stakes litigation. She is known for meticulous preparation, composed courtroom delivery, and an ability to cut through complexity with clarity.

Alice actively serves as amicus curiae in matters of public importance and contributes to legal aid programmes across Maharashtra. Her credo: every client — regardless of means — deserves fearless, informed, and relentless representation.
